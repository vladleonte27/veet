FAQ

Questions, answered.

How is Veet different from cloud dictation?

Veet runs entirely on your machine. Your audio and text never leave your computer. No cloud, no API key, no per-second billing. You own your data.

Do I need a GPU?

No. Veet auto-detects your hardware. On NVIDIA cards it runs ~30× realtime. On any other PC it falls back to CPU mode — slower but works on every Windows 10/11 machine.

Does it work offline?

Yes, after first install. The transcription model downloads once. After that, Veet works with no internet connection.

Which apps does it work with?

All of them. Veet pastes into whichever app you were just using — Slack, Gmail, Notion, Word, your IDE, the address bar, anywhere you can type.

When will Mac and iOS arrive?

Mac is on the immediate roadmap. iOS is more complex (Apple restricts global hotkeys) and will follow. Hit the iOS button above to join the waitlist.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — one click from your account page. No annual contracts, no prorated nonsense.
