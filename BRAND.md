# Veet — Brand Identity

## The mark
A V where the apex is touched by a single cyan dot.
The V is the wordmark initial. The dot is your voice — arriving at the machine.

Files:
- `assets/icon.svg` — vector master
- `assets/icon.png` — 1024×1024 PNG with backdrop
- `assets/icon-mark.png` — 1024×1024 transparent mark for marketing
- `assets/icon.ico` — Windows multi-res (16/24/32/48/64/128/256) for `.exe` + installer
- Generated by `packaging/gen_icons.py` — re-run after tweaks

## Voice

- **Terse.** Cut words.
- **Direct.** Say what happens, not what could.
- **Confident.** No "please" or "kindly". No emoji unless asked.
- **Specific.** Numbers, not adjectives.

Yes: *"Hold to talk."*
No:  *"Please press and hold the keyboard shortcut to begin transcription."*

## Tagline

> **Voice typing, locally.**

Alternates:
- "Hold. Talk. Done."
- "Your voice. Your machine."
- "Type at the speed of speech."

## Don't

- Don't gradient the V mark — single fill only.
- Don't change the dot color from `#67E8F9`.
- Don't pair Veet with another voice-app aesthetic (no waveforms in the lockup, no microphone icons).
- Don't write "VEET" in all caps — it's "Veet".
